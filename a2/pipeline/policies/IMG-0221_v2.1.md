TITLE: Lumbar spine MRI medical necessity (CPT 72148)

M1. At least 6 weeks of conservative therapy (physical therapy, NSAIDs, or activity modification) with failure to improve, documented.
M2. Objective neurological deficit or radicular signs on examination (e.g., positive straight-leg raise, dermatomal sensory loss, motor weakness).
M3. Absence of red flags requiring urgent workup (fever, significant trauma, history of malignancy, cauda equina symptoms). Red flags route to urgent pathway instead.
