VERSION: v1.3
EFFECTIVE: 2025-08-01
TITLE: Chest CT with contrast for pulmonary nodule follow-up (CPT 71260)

N1. Pulmonary nodule documented on prior imaging (chest radiograph or CT).
N2. Requested follow-up interval consistent with Fleischner Society guidance for nodule size and risk category.
N3. No chest CT of the same anatomy within the preceding 3 months (duplicate-imaging check).
