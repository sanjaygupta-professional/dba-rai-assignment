POLICY: RX-1142
VERSION: v4.2
EFFECTIVE: 2026-01-15
TITLE: Biologic immunomodulator step-therapy (RA, PsA, Crohn's disease)

SECTION A — Rheumatoid arthritis (adalimumab, etanercept):
A1. Confirmed diagnosis of moderate-to-severe rheumatoid arthritis documented by a rheumatologist.
A2. Trial of at least 3 months of one or more conventional DMARDs (e.g., methotrexate, sulfasalazine, leflunomide) with inadequate response or intolerance, documented in the clinical record.
A3. Negative tuberculosis screening (QuantiFERON-TB Gold or PPD) within the 12 months preceding the request.

SECTION B — Psoriatic arthritis (etanercept, adalimumab):
B1. Confirmed diagnosis of psoriatic arthritis (CASPAR criteria) documented by a rheumatologist.
B2. Trial of at least 3 months of one conventional DMARD (e.g., methotrexate, sulfasalazine) with inadequate response, verifiable in medication/claims history.
B3. Negative tuberculosis screening within the 12 months preceding the request.

SECTION C — Crohn's disease (infliximab):
C1. Confirmed diagnosis of moderate-to-severe Crohn's disease documented by a gastroenterologist (e.g., SES-CD >= 7 or equivalent severity documentation).
C2. Trial of at least 3 months of conventional therapy (corticosteroid and/or immunomodulator such as azathioprine) with documented inadequate response.
C3. Negative tuberculosis screening within the 12 months preceding the request.
